Our company has a wide specialization.
We do everything: from exterior to interior decoration

Major repairs, interior and exterior decoration, insulation of facades,installation of metal structures and landscaping are labor-intensive and often expensive work. That’s why I want to entrust them to a reliable contractor. The company “ArtHome” offers turnkey services for interior and exterior decoration, insulation of facades.

We do the interior finishing. You can trust us with floor screeding, ceiling finishing, wallpapering, wall puttying, plastering and painting work, plumbing and tiling work. We carry out finishing work in apartments and private houses, office and commercial premises.

We offer installation of metal structures. We carry out the entire range of work: we develop design and working documentation, select materials and construction sites, and control the quality of installation. We create reliable metal structures that last a long time even in difficult climatic zones.

We carry out facade work. We insulate building facades with modern and high-quality materials - mineral wool, polystyrene foam, expanded polystyrene. We insulate the plinths with penoplex and technoplex. We clad facades with brick, clinker tiles, natural and artificial stone.

We are engaged in landscaping. The specialists of the “ArtHome” company have extensive experience in concreting sites and foundations of buildings, laying paving and decorative slabs, designing site designs, creating decorative plantings, laying utility networks and drainage systems.

By choosing a contractor like the “ArtHome” company, you delegate absolutely all issues to specialists. There is no need to think about where to find and how to buy materials profitably. You don't have to look for transport to deliver them. There will be no surplus or shortage of materials - we deliver them in the right quantity. And most importantly, you get a result that suits you.
